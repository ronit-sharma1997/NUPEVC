Our Mission
What We Do

Our aim is to promote awareness of the Private Equity and Venture Capital space and strengthen our local community through educational and hands-on programming. We strive to create connections with peers, faculty, and professionals within the PE/VC atmosphere, in hopes that each member will take away valuable experiences that will serve as catalysts in a potential career in the buy-side industry.

The Private Equity and Venture Capital Club provides students with the opportunity to learn more about the private equity and venture capital industries.

Our Teams
Education
The Education team provides students with a hands on understanding of key concepts in the PE/VC space through a 8-week intensive bootcamp. This course will cover crucial topics to jumpstarting a career in the buy-side industry.
Events
PEVC holds several events each semester in hopes of engaging students with professionals in the space through initiatives such as: fireside chats, panels, case studies. Check out our upcoming events.
Marketing
The Marketing team provides students with opportunities to flex their creativity. Students can design marketing materials or practice professional writing skills through Instagram & weekly newsletters.
Research
The Research team conducts analysis on private markets which is then curated into a biweekly analyst report. Students have the opportunity to specialize in industries that peak their interests and delve deeper into key topics.
